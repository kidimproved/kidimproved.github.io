Our team

Jody Frandle

CEO | Founder | Instructor

Jody has been an elementary school teacher in the San Francisco Unified School District for the past 10 years and is also a National Board Certified Teacher. In addition, she has been performing improv comedy in San Francisco for the past 15 years. Her current troupe, Chicken Scratch, has be a mainstay of the San Francisco Improv Fest for the last 6 years.
